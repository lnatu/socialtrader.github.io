What we’re building...

The platform

Create and build your own personal profile,
community groups, pages, and brands.

Receive news, trade alerts and notifications with
access to public chat and encrypted private
messaging.

A tokenized ecosystem

Traderworm’s vision is to develop a self sustaining
ecosystem that enables users to acquire tokens as
the default currency for services on the platform.

Services included are: advertising, purchasing NFT’s,
promoting new projects, boosting content, and
accessing exclusive VIP groups.

Rewards system

Contributers to the platform are valued at
Traderworm. Therefore offering rewards for helping
build the community through content creating, and
referring users.

Followers are also free to tip their favourite creators.

NFT Marketplace

Having a global audience with access to a
marketplace makes it easier for users to open
discussions while trading their NFT’s.

Traderworm will be minting limited edition collectible
NFT’s for the marketplace.
